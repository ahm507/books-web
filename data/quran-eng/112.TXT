SURAH 112
AL-IKHLAS
Some Attributes of Allah
Revealed at Makkah    
In the Name of Allah,The All-Merciful, The Ever-Merciful.
    The Prophet was once asked about Allah’s attributes. In response to all, Allah revealed some of His attributes: oneness, uniqueness, and other noun substantives.
   
1. Say, Muhammad, “He is Allah and He is Only One;
2. “Allah, The Ever-Lasting Sovereign.
3. “He did not beget nor was He begotten,
4. “And to Him none could be co-equal.”
